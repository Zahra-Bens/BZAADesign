Hello, I am Zahra Bensaidane

Architectural Designer (Environmental Design, UQAM), based in Montreal.

I am an environmental designer driven by a relentless pursuit of innovation, where creativity and logic intertwine seamlessly. My background in mathematics informs my design philosophy, enabling me to explore new geometries and spatial concepts with precision and rigor. Transitioning from the abstract world of reasoning to the dynamic realm of design was a natural progression, allowing me to channel structured thinking into fluid, boundary-pushing creations. Seeking to bridge these two worlds, I pursued studies at UQAM School of Arts, where I learned to merge logical reasoning with artistic expression. In addition to my design work, I am deeply passionate about coding. It empowers me to bring my ideas to life in new and exciting ways. This very website is a product of that passion, designed and coded by me from the ground up. It's not just a showcase of my work, but also an open-source project for those interested in exploring my process. Feel free to check out my GitHub profile for more details. Beyond the digital realm, my hands-on work with artisanal projects allows me to bring tangible, sculptural forms to life, creating spaces and objects that resonate on both a conceptual and sensory level. Through my school projects, I strived to redefine the relationship between form, space, and experience, constantly pushing the boundaries of what design can achieve.
